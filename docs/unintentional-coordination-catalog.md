transcripts). Eight families of price elevation from LOCAL optimization with
no collusion and often no awareness, each mapped to this market's measured
facts. Benchmarks throughout: tacit collusion (retaliate-then-return, fixed
leader, trending margins) and Brown-MacKay (frozen fast=cheap ranking,
clock-regular, 10-30% premia).

## The three cross-cutting discriminators (status)

1. Deviation-response anatomy — RUNNING (pm6): both reabsorption (31%) and
   retaliation-like (51%) present; family-2 vs collusion split already
   quantified; next: noise-fragility (idiosyncratic vs common shocks).
2. Tie anatomy — RUN (pm5 + multiset null): ties at shared levels, author
   identity NOT special => families 4/6 mixture, against barometric and
   against B-M (which forbids ties) for the tied segment.
3. Cohort conditioning — PARTIAL (wf13/14): comovement by shared-tool
   membership and vendor-version dates not yet instrumented -> add repricer
   fingerprint clustering (cut-increment + reset-hour signatures) to the
   watcher set.

## Ranked live-here assessment

Platform-ranking suppression (7, measured) > reference anchoring (4, measured
atoms + calendar) > captive/Diamond structure (1, the wedge) > H-K focal ties
(6) > shared-default monoculture (5, emerging cohort) > learning-without-
strategy (2, cohort candidate) > institutional baseline (8, needs design
variation) > barometric imitation (3, gated on author moves).

Collective implication: at least four measured mechanisms elevate or freeze
prices here with ZERO coordination intent — the null against which any
collusion claim must now be argued is not Bertrand but this stack.
